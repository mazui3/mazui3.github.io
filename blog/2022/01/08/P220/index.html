Customer Success

Customer success is a method for ensuring customers reach their desired outcomes when using an organization’s product or service. A relationship-focused customer success strategy includes involvement in the purchase decision, implementation and use of products or services and customer support.
大概是保障自己的产品是否真的在客户里有效被使用,考虑客户体验的职位吧.

One of the most commonly asked questions that we get is what exactly is the difference between customer success and customer support? A distinct difference: proactive versus reactive responses.
Customer success teams tend to focus on more high level issues, such as running a smooth onboarding process, directing customers to new areas of the product they haven’t checked out yet, or resolving issues that are blocking an upsell or renewal opportunity.

Upselling typically refers to the encouragement of the customer to purchase an additional feature or functionality as a part of the initial product, while cross-selling tends to refer to the encouragement of the customer to purchase a tandem product that would improve their experience, but isn’t necessarily a part of the base product.
