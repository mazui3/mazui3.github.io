Let $\alpha$ be the attribute of interest, and let $t_u = T_{\alpha}(y_u)$ be the power-transformed observation for unit $u$. Let us also define the objective function to be minimized as the absolute value of Pearson‚Äôs moment coefficient of skewness for the transformed data: $\rho(\alpha;\mathcal{P}) = \left|\frac{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{u \in \mathcal{P}}\left(t_u-\bar{t}\right)^3}{[SD(t)]^3}\right|$
